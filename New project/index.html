About Us

Built for Students, Powered by Verified Student Voices

Our Mission

To remove chaos from student decision-making by offering trusted, hyperlocal and structured insights for every major part of campus life.

What We Solve

Freshers currently depend on rumors, random threads and spam groups to find PGs, clubs or events. Local Saarthi creates one reliable, data-backed system.

Who We Serve

Freshers, current students, student societies, event organizers, and hyperlocal businesses that want to reach the right campus audience.

Product

Three Core Pillars of Local Saarthi

1. Societies & Clubs

Ratings on CV value, work culture, leadership quality, and alumni network strength.

2. Hyperlocal Amenities

Trusted ratings for PGs, canteens and hangout spots with focus on student budget and safety.

3. Events & Fests

Centralized campus calendar with hype scores, attendance feedback, and recommendations.

Our Story

Built by Students, for Students

"Local Saarthi was built to solve a simple problem - students deserve trustworthy information about their campus life."

We are starting with PG discovery - the most painful problem for students relocating to a new city. From there, we are expanding into cafes, student societies, and campus events to build a complete campus life platform.
